Our Company

It is our company's mission to make going back to school easier than ever before! The Magic School Bus removes all the stress from back-to-school shopping with our curated 'survival kits.' We offer kits for all ages, from kindergarten to your favorite coding bootcamp!

The MSB Team

The MSB Team's founding members Tamela, Harper, and Bri all met during their time as students in coding bootcamp. They all bonded over the fact that they were all human, and in turn, very needy. They also realized that other students in their class also had basic human needs. They decided right then and there that they would spend the rest of their lives creating a brand that would respond to this need all humans have. MSB.com creates an opportunity for family members and friends to be creative and to say "I care about you," "I miss you," and "I am thinking about you."


Our Formula

Quality products for each care package are carefully chosen here at MSB.com. We have tailored each package to meet unique needs for each category through an algorithm based on Maslow's hierarchy of needs. For example, the coding bootcamp package includes a travel coffee mug, a critical time in one's life to have access to caffeine any and all times.

The Total Package

MSB.com offers a unique service that allows consumers to save time by choosing from a menu of Ready Made Packages. Sending a MSB is a great way to show someone you CARE! MSB.com strives to make communicating through mail as fun and gratifying as possible for both sender and receiver. We utilize the Internet to create a convenient service for consumers to make a selection from our collection of products, write a custom message, and ship a MSB. In a few minutes time you can make someone feel special and reap the benefits of your good will!

Give us your money and we guarantee that you will be prepared for any academic obstacles heading your way!HOORAY SCHOOL!!!!
